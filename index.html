Trusted Funeral Service

Trusted by thousands in Caraga Administrative Region for over 100 years, St. Kalot Funeral Services offers a wide range of life plans that are suitable to your needs. We also provide assistance for any funeral-related concerns.

Customised Life Plans

Secure your future and ease the burden on your loved ones with our customised life plans. We offer a range of options designed to meet your unique needs and budget, ensuring that your wishes are honored even in your final moments.

Customised Caskets

Design a resting place that truly reflects your individuality. Choose from our pre-made caskets or personalised touched, allowing you to choose materials, designs, and details that resonate with your life story.

E-Burial

Ensure that distance doesn't prevent your loved ones from being part of your final journey. Our online burial services provide a live-streamed, virtual experience, allowing family and friends to see you for the last time wherever they are.

Prayer Group

Honor your loved ones with a dedicated group who lead nightly prayers and novenas, providing spiritual support and solace during the wake and beyond.

Wake Games

Embrace a time-honored Filipino custom with our Wake Games service, where friends and family gather for friendly games of chance.
